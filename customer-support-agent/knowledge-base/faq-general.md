# General FAQs - Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Account & Login Issues

### How do I reset my password?
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a reset link (arrives within 5 minutes)
5. Click the link and create a new password

**Note:** Reset links expire after 24 hours. If you don't see the email, check spam/junk folders.

### Can I change my email address?
Yes. Log in to your account → Settings → Account Information → Update Email. You'll need to verify the new email address.

### My account is locked. What should I do?
Accounts are automatically locked after 5 failed login attempts for security. The lock expires after 30 minutes, or you can reset your password immediately to regain access.

### Can I have multiple accounts?
Our terms of service allow one account per person. If you need to manage multiple profiles (personal + business), contact support for our business account options.

## Orders & Shipping

### How long does shipping take?
- **Standard Shipping:** 5-7 business days
- **Express Shipping:** 2-3 business days  
- **Overnight:** Next business day (orders placed before 2 PM EST)

Shipping times begin after the order leaves our warehouse, typically 1-2 business days after order placement.

### How can I track my order?
You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. Click the tracking link in the email or log into your account → Order History → View Tracking.

### Can I change my shipping address after ordering?
If the order hasn't shipped yet (status shows "Processing"), we can update the address. Contact support immediately. Once shipped, we cannot redirect the package, but you may be able to contact the carrier directly.

### What if my package shows "delivered" but I didn't receive it?
1. Check with household members
2. Look around your property (porches, side doors, garages)
3. Check with neighbors
4. Contact your building's mailroom (if applicable)

If you still can't locate it after 24 hours, contact support for assistance. We may file a carrier claim or send a replacement.

### Do you ship internationally?
Currently we ship to the US, Canada, and select European countries. Enter your address at checkout to see if we deliver to your location.

## Returns & Exchanges

### What is your return policy?
**30-day money-back guarantee** on most items. Products must be:
- Returned within 30 days of delivery
- In original condition (unused, unworn, undamaged)
- In original packaging when possible

See our full Return & Refund Policy document for details.

### How do I start a return?
1. Log into your account → Order History
2. Select the order → Click "Return Item"
3. Choose your reason for return
4. Print the prepaid return label
5. Ship the item back within 7 days

### Are returns free?
Returns are free for defective items or our mistakes. For other returns, a $7.99 restocking fee applies, deducted from your refund.

### How long do refunds take?
Once we receive your return:
- **Processing time:** 2-3 business days to inspect and approve
- **Refund time:** 5-7 business days to appear in your account (varies by bank/card issuer)

You'll receive email confirmation when your refund is processed.

### Can I exchange instead of return?
Yes! Follow the same return process but select "Exchange" and choose your new item. We'll ship the exchange as soon as we receive your return.

## Payment & Billing

### What payment methods do you accept?
- All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Affirm (for orders over $100)

### Is my payment information secure?
Yes. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and never store complete credit card numbers. All payments are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment processors.

### Why was my payment declined?
Common reasons:
- Insufficient funds
- Incorrect billing address
- Card expired
- Bank fraud prevention (contact your bank)
- International cards not enabled for US purchases

Try a different payment method or contact your bank to authorize the transaction.

### Can I use multiple payment methods for one order?
Currently we only accept one payment method per order. Consider using a service like PayPal that allows you to split payment across multiple funding sources.

### Do you offer payment plans?
Yes! Orders over $100 qualify for Affirm, which offers flexible payment plans. Select Affirm at checkout to see your options.

## Product Information

### Are your products authentic?
Yes. We are an authorized retailer for all brands we carry. Every product comes directly from the manufacturer or authorized distributor.

### What if I receive a defective product?
Contact support immediately with:
- Order number
- Photos of the defect
- Description of the issue

We'll send a replacement immediately or issue a full refund (including return shipping). Our quality guarantee covers defects for 90 days.

### Do products come with a warranty?
Most products include the manufacturer's warranty. Warranty length and coverage vary by product - check the product page or packaging for details. We can help facilitate warranty claims.

### Can you help me choose the right product?
Absolutely! Provide details about:
- What you need the product for
- Your budget
- Any specific requirements

Our support team can make personalized recommendations.
