# Return & Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025

## Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy for any reason, we offer a **30-day money-back guarantee** on most products.

---

## Return Window

### Standard Returns
- **Timeline:** 30 days from delivery date
- **Condition:** Items must be unused, unworn, and in original condition
- **Packaging:** Original packaging preferred but not required for most items

### Extended Holiday Returns
- **November 1 - December 31:** Items purchased during this period can be returned until January 31 of the following year
- All other return conditions still apply

---

## Eligible Items

### What Can Be Returned

✅ **Most products purchased directly from us:**
- Unopened items in original condition
- Defective or damaged items (any condition)
- Wrong items shipped by us (any condition)
- Items that don't match product description

### What Cannot Be Returned

❌ **For health, safety, or hygiene reasons:**
- Intimate apparel and swimwear (unless defective)
- Personal care items that have been opened
- Earrings and body jewelry
- Face masks

❌ **Special order and personalized items:**
- Custom or made-to-order products
- Engraved or monogrammed items
- Items marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase

❌ **Digital products:**
- Downloadable software
- Digital gift cards (once redeemed)
- E-books and digital content

## Return Process

### Step 1: Initiate Return
**Online:**
1. Log into your account at [website]
2. Go to Order History
3. Select the order
4. Click "Return Items"
5. Choose items to return and reason

**Without Account or Receipt:**
Email support@[company].com with:
- Your name and order number (if available)
- Description of items to return
- Reason for return

### Step 2: Prepare Your Return
1. Pack items securely in original packaging (if available)
2. Include the return slip (printed from your return request)
3. DO NOT include the original shipping label

**Packing Tips:**
- Use appropriate padding to prevent damage in transit
- Ensure items are clean and in sellable condition
- Remove any personal belongings from pockets/compartments

### Step 3: Ship Your Return

**With Prepaid Label (Provided for most returns):**
1. Print the prepaid return label
2. Affix it to the outside of your package
3. Drop off at any [carrier] location

**For International Returns:**
Contact support for specific return instructions. International return shipping may not be prepaid.

### Step 4: Track Your Return
You'll receive email updates at each stage:
- ✅ Return initiated
- ✅ Return received at warehouse
- ✅ Return inspected and approved
- ✅ Refund processed

## Refund Information

### Refund Timeline

**From when we receive your return:**
1. **Inspection:** 2-3 business days to verify condition
2. **Processing:** Refund initiated same day as approval
3. **Bank/Card Processing:** 5-7 business days to appear in your account

Total time: Typically 7-14 days from when we receive the item.

### Refund Method

**Original Payment Method:**
- Refunds are issued to your original payment method
- Credit card refunds appear as a credit on your statement
- PayPal refunds return to your PayPal balance
- Buy Now, Pay Later (Affirm) - your payment plan will be adjusted

**Store Credit Option:**
- You can request store credit instead of a refund
- Store credit is issued immediately upon return approval
- 10% bonus applied to store credit refunds (e.g., $100 refund = $110 store credit)

### Refund Amount

**Full Refund Scenarios:**
- Defective or damaged items: **100% refund + free return shipping**
- Our shipping error: **100% refund + free return shipping**
- Item doesn't match description: **100% refund + free return shipping**

**Standard Returns:**
- **Item cost:** Full refund
- **Restocking fee:** $7.99 deducted from refund
- **Original shipping:** Not refunded (unless we made an error)
- **Return shipping:** Prepaid label provided (fee deducted from refund)

**Partial Refunds:**
Items returned in less than perfect condition may receive:
- 50% refund: Item used/worn but resellable
- 25% refund: Item damaged but salvageable  
- No refund: Item unsellable due to damage/condition

## Exchanges

### How Exchanges Work

We don't offer direct exchanges. To exchange an item:

1. **Return the original item** following the return process
2. **Place a new order** for the item you want

**Why we do it this way:**
- Faster processing (new order ships immediately)
- No waiting for original item to arrive back
- You can choose different size, color, or product
- Original return refund issued once received

### Exchange Discount

To make up for the inconvenience:
- Use code **EXCHANGE10** for 10% off your new order
- Code valid for 30 days after your return is initiated
- Can be combined with items already on sale

## Special Circumstances

### Defective Items

**Our Quality Guarantee:**
If you receive a defective item:
1. Contact support immediately with photos
2. No need to return the defective item (in most cases)
3. Replacement shipped immediately at no cost
4. Valid for 90 days after purchase

**What qualifies as defective:**
- Manufacturing defects
- Items damaged in shipping
- Items that don't function as intended
- Missing parts or components

### Wrong Item Received

If we shipped the wrong item:
1. Contact support with order number and photo
2. We'll send the correct item immediately
3. Keep the wrong item until the correct one arrives
4. Prepaid return label provided for wrong item
5. Full refund if correct item is unavailable

### Lost Returns

If your return tracking shows delivered but we haven't received it:
1. Allow 3 business days for warehouse processing
2. Contact support if still not showing as received
3. We'll open a carrier investigation
4. Refund will be processed pending investigation results

### Damaged Returns

If items are damaged during return shipping:
- **Proper packaging:** We'll still process your refund
- **Improper packaging:** Refund may be adjusted based on damage

**Tips to avoid issues:**
- Use sturdy box with adequate padding
- Remove old shipping labels
- Get tracking and insurance for high-value items

## Return Fraud Prevention

We trust our customers, but to prevent abuse:

**Multiple returns may trigger:**
- Account review if >50% of purchases are returned
- Temporary hold on future returns
- Account closure in extreme cases of return fraud

**We reserve the right to refuse returns that:**
- Show signs of excessive use or damage
- Are missing original tags or packaging (when required)
- Appear to violate our terms (e.g., "wardrobing")

This protects honest customers and keeps prices fair.
