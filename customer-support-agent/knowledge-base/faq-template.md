## Billing & Pricing

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:
- All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- PayPal
- ACH bank transfer (for annual plans)
- Wire transfer (for enterprise customers)

All payments are processed securely through [payment processor].

### When will I be charged?

Billing depends on your plan:

- **Monthly plans**: Charged on the same day each month
- **Annual plans**: Charged once per year on your signup anniversary
- **Free trials**: No charge during trial. First payment occurs when trial ends (you'll receive an email reminder 3 days before)

You can view your next billing date in Settings → Billing.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Settings → Billing
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Confirm cancellation

- You'll have access until the end of your current billing period
- No refunds for partial months (monthly plans)
- Annual plans: see our refund policy below

### What is your refund policy?

**Monthly subscriptions**: No refunds for partial months, but you can cancel anytime and keep access until the period ends.

**Annual subscriptions**: 
- Full refund if canceled within 30 days
- Pro-rated refund for first 90 days
- No refunds after 90 days

**Exception**: Accounts terminated for policy violations are not eligible for refunds.

### Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can change plans anytime:

**Upgrading**:
- Takes effect immediately
- Pro-rated charge for the difference
- New features available right away

**Downgrading**:
- Takes effect at next billing cycle
- No charges until then
- Keep current features until downgrade

To change plans: Settings → Billing → Change Plan
