## Common Troubleshooting

### "I can't access [Feature X]"

**Possible causes:**
1. **Plan limitation:** [Feature X] is only available on [Plan tier] and above
   - Solution: Upgrade your plan or request access
2. **Permissions:** Your account role doesn't have access
   - Solution: Ask your account admin to grant permission
3. **Feature flag:** This feature may be in beta or not yet enabled
   - Solution: Contact support to request access

### "Why is [Action] not working?"

**Common issues:**
1. **Browser cache:** Old data may be cached
   - Solution: Clear cache and refresh (Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + R)
2. **Concurrent limit reached:** Maximum simultaneous [actions] exceeded
   - Solution: Wait for current actions to complete or upgrade plan
3. **File size/format:** File doesn't meet requirements
   - Solution: Check file specifications above

### "[Feature Y] is slow/timing out"

**Likely causes:**
1. **Large data set:** Processing [X] items can take time
   - Expected: Up to [X] minutes for [Y] items
   - Solution: Break into smaller batches or schedule for off-peak
2. **Network connectivity:** Connection may be unstable
   - Solution: Check internet connection and retry
3. **System load:** High traffic periods (typically [times])
   - Solution: Try again in a few minutes or during off-peak hours
